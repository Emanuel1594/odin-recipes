Bacon-asparagus dippers with jammy eggs

Description

Three simple ingredients are all you need to make this fingers-only breakfast! Bacon-wrapped asparagus "dippers" are the perfect accompaniment for jammy eggs — aka soft-boiled eggs. This higher-satiety meal has only 3 grams of net carbs per serving — a playful and filling way to start your day. Dip away!

Ingredients

Bacon-asparagus dippers

  • 6 oz. slices of bacon
  • 7 oz. of green asparagus

Jammy eggs

  • 4 large eggs

Instructions

Bacon asparagus dippers

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  2. Slice each rasher of bacon in half lengthways.
  3. Start at one end of each asparagus spear and wrap a strip of bacon along the length of the asparagus. Place on the baking tray. Repeat with the remaining asparagus and bacon.
  4. Bake for 10-15 minutes until the bacon is crispy and the asparagus is tender.

Jammy eggs

  1. Bring a pot of water to boil over high heat. Carefully poke a tiny hole into the bottom of each egg with an egg pricker or a safety pin.
  2. When the water is boiling, lower the eggs into the water and boil for 6 minutes.
  3. After 6 minutes, rinse the eggs in cold water. With a sharp knife, crack the top of the egg and cut it off.

To serve

  1. Serve the eggs and dippers together on a plate. There are 2 eggs and 6 bacon-asparagus dippers per serving.
